VISDEV

visual / storytelling / development

MISSION

STATEMENT

VisDev is an international visual storytelling development server created in 2015. It stands at more than a hundred members who are artists, writers, coders, musicians, photographers, and more. We welcome a variety of relevant skills and specializations.

Put simply, we create.

Our server focuses on improvement and collaboration. We are a community that thrives on idea exchange and fostering a self-driven educational environment. Monthly workshops provide our users a platform to share their knowledge. Members critique and encourage one another on their respective path to self-improvement in dedicated channels of skillset.

MEMBERSHIP

APPLICATION

Interested in joining us? Fill in the provided form below, which will be examined by our moderators. Don’t worry about impressing us; we’re not judging your abilities, but trying to evaluate how well you’d fit in our community. We’re looking for people with a strong work ethic and a desire to improve, no matter their current skill level. New applicants are processed weekly, please be patient.
